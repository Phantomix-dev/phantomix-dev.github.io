Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2026

At Phantomix, we believe that your digital life should be simple, safe, and entirely yours. This Privacy Policy outlines how our tools operate and how we protect your data.

1. Zero-Data Architecture (Client-Side Processing)

The core philosophy of Phantomix is "Your Data Stays on Your Device."

  • Photo Privacy Cleaner: When you use our EXIF cleaner, the image processing happens entirely in your browser. We do not upload, view, or store your photos on any external servers.
  • Password Vault: Passwords are generated locally on your machine using standard cryptographic algorithms. They are never transmitted over the internet.
  • Private Note Sender: Our burner protocols are designed to self-destruct upon reading, ensuring no logs or histories are retained by us.

2. Local Storage (DiamondMax Ecosystem)

For gamified experiences like the DiamondMax Reward Hub, we use your browser's Local Storage to save your points and progress. This means your data lives on your device, not on a central database. Clearing your browser cache may reset this data.

3. Third-Party Analytics

To understand how users interact with our platform and to improve our tools, we use basic analytics services (such as Google Analytics). This data is anonymized and aggregated. It does not include personally identifiable information (PII).

4. No Advertising Trackers

We are committed to providing a clean, ad-free experience. Phantomix does not inject third-party advertising cookies or hidden trackers into your browser.

5. Changes to This Policy

As we roll out new features and transition from our Alpha Demo to a full release, we may update this policy. We encourage you to review it periodically.

Phantomix: Built for privacy, designed for humans.
