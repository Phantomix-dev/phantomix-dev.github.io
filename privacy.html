Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2026

At Phantomix, we believe that your digital life should be simple, safe, and entirely yours. This Privacy Policy outlines how our tools operate, what data we collect (and what we strictly don't), and how we protect your information across the Phantomix Ecosystem and DiamondMax App.

1. Zero-Data Architecture (Client-Side Tools)

The core philosophy for our utility tools is "Your Data Stays on Your Device."

  • Photo Privacy Cleaner: Image processing happens entirely in your browser/device. We do not upload, view, or store your photos on any external servers.
  • Password Vault: Passwords are generated locally on your machine using standard cryptographic algorithms. They are never transmitted over the internet.
  • Private Note Sender: Our burner protocols are designed to self-destruct upon reading, ensuring no logs or histories are retained by us.

2. Account & Authentication Data

To provide community access and exclusive DiamondMax features, we require account registration.

  • What we collect: We collect your Email Address and a securely hashed password when you join our Waitlist or create a Node (Account).
  • How it is protected: We utilize bank-grade encryption via Supabase (our backend provider). We cannot see your actual password.
  • Data Deletion: You have the right to request the complete deletion of your account and associated email at any time by contacting our support.

3. Local Storage (DiamondMax Ecosystem)

For gamified experiences like the DiamondMax Reward Hub, we use your browser's Local Storage to save specific points and progress. This means your operational data lives on your device, minimizing server-side tracking. Clearing your app cache may reset non-synced data.

4. Third-Party Analytics & Permissions

To understand how users interact with our platform and to improve our tools, we may use basic analytics services. This data is anonymized and aggregated. It does not include personally identifiable information (PII).

For Android (APK) Users: The DiamondMax app will only request permissions strictly necessary for its functionality (e.g., storage access for saving local files). We do not request unnecessary background tracking permissions.

5. No Advertising Trackers

We are committed to providing a clean, secure experience. Phantomix does not inject third-party advertising cookies, hidden trackers, or sell your data to marketing agencies.
